Some of the courses we offer

Fundamentals of Data Analytics

Description

Data Analytics is rapidly becoming one of the most critical drivers for any decision-making, at an individual or a business level. At the heart of Data analytics, lies the fundamentals of statistics. This course will help you learn basic statistical concepts with practical problem solving and interpretation through application of the theoretical learnings. The course has been divided into 3 modules with Simulations, Quizzes and Solve within each of those.

Learning Objective

Learn fundamental statistical concepts, that are widely applicable in data analytics through course lessons and solving business cases. Apply the knowledge gained to solve business problems through simulations using real data, validate your knowledge by answering quiz questions under each module and finally test your understanding by solving real problems under the Solve section. At the end of this course, you should be able to understand data type and their representation, apply descriptive statistical measures to interpret data and make statistical inferences based on the data distribution and use of appropriate statistical tests.

Data Mining Concepts and Techniques

Description

Structured in several modules covering a couple of business cases and exercises, the learner gets the opportunities of hands-on applications of all the data mining methodologies widely applicable in the industry covering below.

  • Data preparation and sampling
  • Data quality assessment and necessary treatment
  • Data transformation and feature extraction
  • Dimensionality reduction


After completing this course, one will get a profound understanding of all the stages of data preparations required for application of analytical methodologies to address real business challenges.

This course is planned to equip a learner with all the necessary concepts, techniques, approaches, and data mining skills required by a data scientist prior to the application of predictive and prescriptive analytics. It imparts the CRISP DM methodologies and it's significance across different phases of data-mining.

Mining Text Data - Cleansing, Treatment, Structural Representation & Visualization

Description

Text mining & analytics is one of the fastest-growing applied streams in the analytics industry. In real business scenarios, the text data, in majority of the cases is unstructured, not cleansed, and not treated. Hence, the unstructured text data is not in a shape to use for any kind of insight generation, business analytics application, or leverage for decision support (predictive or prescriptive). It is of utmost importance to prepare the data prior to any kind of text analytics.

Learning Objective

This course is designed to equip a learner with all the necessary concepts and skills required to mine, explore, cleanse, and prepare unstructured text data and make it ready for analyses.

Sentiment Analysis - Using Unstructured Text Data

Description

"Text Analytics" is one of the fastest-growing applied streams in the analytics industry. This course helps in understanding the basic concepts of comparative analysis using distance metrics, and sentiment analysis, using a vocabulary method. The first module of this course focuses on comparative analysis of text documents to rate and review unstructured text from two different sources. This module covers the concepts of "Dissimilarity between documents" and "Distance measures". The concepts learned in this course allows one to verify the credibility of information from one source, using a credible source as the reference.

Learning Objective

Learn the fundamental steps involved in exploratory Text Analytics by comparing two documents and finding out their various distance measures. Learn to classify a document of reviews based on sentiment.

Concepts and application of objective and subjective Segmentation

Description

Text Analytics - Classification and Clustering

Description

Text classification and clustering form an integral part of analysing text data. In most business scenarios, it is important to analyse the text as a whole and as part of certain subgroups, which might not be apparent from simple frequency analysis. This is where clustering becomes necessary. Text Clustering helps one identify the underlying themes in text data, while also suggesting the number of themes that might be present in the data

Learning Objective

This course is designed to equip a learner with all the necessary concepts and skills required to cluster and classify text data, while also validating if the model is a good fit using training-validation sets.

Fundamentals of Time Series Analysis

Description

Analyttica TreasureHunt presents a bouquet of courses designed for individuals who want to learn the art of analysing time series data. This course, the first one in the series, introduces the learner to the concept of time series data and basic analysis with this data. The techniques discussed in this course are the building blocks of time series analysis and makes an individual ready for further exploration of various high end techniques of time series forecasting.

Learning Objective

Structured in 3 modules and 6 real life business cases, the learner is given a hands-on exposure to various basic analysis ranging from visualisation using time series plots and smoothing, to analysing seasonal components and removing trends. The course also introduces the concept of auto-correlation function and partial auto-correlation function, a major tool for time series forecasting.
